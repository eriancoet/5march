Welcome to the African Elephant Restaurant

The African Elephant Restaurant is your go-to destination for the most authentic South African cuisine in Sabie, South Africa. Located at 8 Trichardt Street, our restaurant is the perfect spot to satisfy your cravings for delicious food while immersing yourself in the local culture.

At the African Elephant, we take pride in offering a wide range of dishes that are made using the freshest and most flavorful ingredients. Our menu is filled with classic South African dishes, each of which is prepared with great care and attention to detail.

So come and join us at the African Elephant and experience the true flavors of South African cuisine. We look forward to serving you!

In addition to our delicious food, we also offer a warm and welcoming atmosphere that is perfect for any occasion. Whether you're looking to enjoy a meal with your family or celebrate a special event with friends, the African Elephant is the perfect place to do so.
