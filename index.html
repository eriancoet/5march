Welcome to the African Elephant Restaurant

The African Elephant Restaurant, a family-friendly eatery located at 8 Trichardt Street in Sabie, South Africa, offers the most authentic South African cuisine using the freshest and most flavorful ingredients. Our menu boasts classic South African dishes prepared with great care and attention to detail, ensuring that every bite is an unforgettable experience. With a warm and welcoming atmosphere perfect for any occasion, the African Elephant is the ultimate destination for those seeking a truly satisfying and memorable dining experience. Come and join us to indulge in the true flavors of South African cuisine, and let us serve you with the warmth and hospitality that makes us a family restaurant.
