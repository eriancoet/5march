About the African Elephant Restaurant

The African Elephant Restaurant is a proudly South African establishment that serves authentic, home-style cuisine in Sabie, Mpumalanga. Since 2014, we have been passionate about sharing the flavors and traditions of South Africa with our customers through our menu, which features a variety of locally-sourced dishes prepared with care and attention to detail. With a warm and welcoming atmosphere, our restaurant is the perfect spot to relax and enjoy a meal with loved ones, whether you're a local or a visitor to the area. Come and experience the true tastes of South Africa at the African Elephant Restaurant.
